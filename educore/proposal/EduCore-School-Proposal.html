The Problem

Running a school still means juggling a dozen disconnected tools

Most schools patch together spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, paper registers, and manual bank reconciliation — and every gap between those tools costs time, money, and trust with parents.

📋
Paper-based records

Admission files, attendance registers, and report cards live in cabinets — slow to retrieve, easy to lose, impossible to analyze.

💸
Manual fee collection

Tracking who has paid, chasing defaulters, and reconciling bank transfers by hand eats into staff time every single term.

📵
Disconnected communication

Parents find out about school closures, fee deadlines, or their child's performance late — or not at all.

🧾
Slow, error-prone admissions

Walk-in applications, handwritten forms, and no way for parents to track application status remotely.
